Ayush is very interested in archery, so he goes to the sports club to practice his skills. Today he was given a unique task to enhance his skills, and he was given a n*n grid as a target. If he aims at a grid and successfully aims, he cannot strike the grid that occupies the immediate top, bottom, left, or right position.
Your task here is to find the maximum number of grids that he can aim for.
Input format 
T ---> number of test case
N ---> grid size
Constraints 
1<=T<=100;
2<=N<=6*10^7
Output Format
Print the output corresponding to each test case on a new line.
